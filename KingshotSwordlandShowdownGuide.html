📚 Quick Guide

  • No Losses: All troops heal free – fight aggressive!
  • Pre-battle Preparation:
    • Recall all marches home. Heal infirmary 100%
    • Activate buffs/gear/heroes, use Howard, Gordon, Quinn, and Amadeus for defense
    • Set defense march presets for 70% infantry/30% cavalry/0% archers (if possible)
    • Join Discord voice (English speakers) 5 minutes before start (commit full 60 mins)
  • In-game Freebies
    • 5 free initial march speedups (regenerate 1/min, max 30)
    • 1 free initial teleport (regenerate every 10 min --> 5 min w/ Stables, max 1). Plan, don't spam!
    • Initial free healing speedups (regenerate ?/min, max unknown). If you're a whale and get wiped, use the 12-minute healing reset trick 👇🏽
    • Healing Reset: Tap scoreboard banner (top screen) --> "Leave Battlefield" --> free full heal --> rejoin after 12 min cooldown
  • City Burn Tactic: If a city is destroyed --> garrisoned troops teleport home with city (resets their garrisoned troops back to their city)
  • Counter the Burn Tactic:
    • Troop Dance to dodge incoming attacks: send city troops to camp next to you --> recall --> teleport away safe. Garrisons stay!
    • Spread out – don't cluster. Garrisoned fish are safer spread between buildings, not next to them. Starve the enemy of teleports ⚡
  • Coordinate: Discord: "Squad A Garrison Stables" "Squad C Rally Hall" Whales ping/tag
  • If Behind: Guerrilla – burn, scoop supplies, under-cellars, flip quick for first-capture bonuses

Game Strategy

💭 3 Whale-Led Squads (~8 per Squad from Legions)

  • Squad A 🐳 Whale1 owns Royal Stables + 1 Sanctum + 2 Abbeys
  • Squad B 🐳 Whale2 owns Bell Tower + 1 Sanctum + 2 Abbeyslies
  • Squad C 🐳 Whale3 owns Merc Camp + Hall of Ref, phase 3 Swordshrine rallies

Squad Strategy (everyone)

  • Whales lead captures/rallies with 1st march. 2nd/3rd march for destroying nearby enemy cities, defending secondary buildings, & defending squad cities
  • Fish join your squad's rallies and garrison/reinforce your squad's buildings first from a short safe distance. 2nd march for defending your squad's cities and collecting supplies
  • Garrison/reinforce buildings with the "Tank" troop ratio (70% infantry/30% cavalry/0% archers) when possible

🐟 Basics for Fish (Defenders/Supports)

Your Role: Hold the line! Follow your Whale.

  1. Teleport: To your squad's safe zone (R4 will mark). Stay just out of sight but near your squad's buildings, don't cluster together
  2. March Speedups: Use free ones to arrive FAST for garrisons.
  3. Garrison Everything: After whale captures --> garrison IMMEDIATELY
  4. Reinforce: ALWAYS join ally garrisons. Stack > solo
  5. Scoop Arsenal Supplies: Every drop = points. Prioritize supplies capture after garrisons secure
  6. Spare Marches: Pick up supplies, capture under-cellars, reinforce other squad members

Never: Solo attack, cluster near other squad members or buildings, exit buildings that you've reinforced (unless asked)

🐳 Basics for the 3 Whales (Attack Leaders)

Your Role: Capture, rally, burn, disrupt. Use 1st march main, 2nd march for burns/defense

  1. Teleport/March: Talk to your squad, let them know when you launch rallys or need support
  2. Phase 1: Rush capture your squad buildings (Stables/Bell/Sanctums/Abbeys). Garrison quick --> clear out invaders --> move to help others
  3. Burns: 2nd march on enemy cities near you. Force their garrisons home!
  4. Phase 2: Rally Hall first (buffs). Then Swordshrine (you START, squad joins). Send Mercs on enemy holds
  5. Phase 3: Push Swordshrine rallies (first capture gives a big bonus). Hunt under-cellars w/ squad. Rally enemy buildings for supply drops that fish can collect
  6. Rotations: Healing Reset/Troop Dance to stay strong
  7. City Killers: You + dolphins = burn squad. Target enemy whales first
